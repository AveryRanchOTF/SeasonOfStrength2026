What Is Season of Strength?

An 8-week strength focused challenge designed to help you build muscle, increase strength, and see real progress. You'll show up, track your lifts, and get stronger — one rep at a time.

Total weight lifted = 40 lb

Understanding Your Score

Your score is a reference point, not a pass/fail grade.

🔥 More weight + more reps = biggest score gain
↑ More weight, fewer reps = score may go up or down
📈 Less weight/reps = lower score, but can still show progress
👤 Compare yourself to yourself — not others

8-Week Game Plan

Your roadmap to getting stronger

Weeks 1–2
Baseline Strength Score
  • Learn movement quality first
  • Update your Strength Score Card
  • Focus on consistent reps and form
Weeks 3–5
Progressive Overload
Try ONE of these each week:
  • Increase weight slightly
  • Add 1–2 reps
  • Improve tempo/control
Weeks 6–7
Push Performance
  • Challenge heavier weights safely
  • Prioritize recovery
Week 8
Re-Test & Celebrate 🎉
Compare your:
  • Lift scores & energy levels
  • Confidence
  • InBody scan changes
  • Workout consistency

3 Pillars of Progress

Gains happen through training + nutrition + recovery working together.

1
Prioritize Protein

Muscle growth requires enough protein to repair and rebuild after workouts.

Aim for 0.7–1.0g of protein per pound of body weight
Plan for pre & post-workout nutrition
2
Rest = Results

Muscle is built during recovery — not during class. Sleep and rest days matter as much as training.

3
Game Plan Your 4 Lifts

Use the template of the day to choose your lifts as the "Finisher." Track every session.
